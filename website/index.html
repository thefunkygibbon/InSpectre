Everything, in one pane of glass

From the moment a device joins your network to the moment you cut it off, InSpectre has it covered.

Instant discovery

Active ARP sweeps plus a passive packet sniffer surface new devices within seconds — no agents, no config.

Vulnerability scanning

Nuclei + nmap CVE scanning per device, with scheduled sweeps and a severity-grouped security dashboard.

One-click blocking

Cut any device off the internet via ARP MITM. Pause the whole network, or schedule recurring blocks.

Person presence

Group a household member's devices under one person, see Home/Away at a glance, and block by person.

Traffic & speed

Per-device traffic analysis — domains, countries, unusual ports — plus scheduled Ookla speed tests.

Live updates

Server-Sent Events push changes to the UI the instant they happen — no jarring full-page refreshes.

Container monitoring

Watch Docker & Proxmox hosts, stream logs, and Trivy-scan images for CVEs — all in the same UI.

Smart fingerprinting

OUI, DHCP and port-pattern matching — with optional Fingerbank lookups — auto-classify every device.

Notifications, your way

Channels & Profiles route any event to ntfy, Gotify, Pushbullet, Slack, Telegram, Discord, HA & more.

People, not just MACs

See who's home at a glance

Group the phone, laptop, watch and tablet a household member carries under a single named person with a photo. InSpectre turns "is any of their kit online?" into a clear Home/Away state.

  • Colour-coded Home / Away cards with presence history
  • Nominate a primary device as the source of truth
  • Block all of a person's devices in one click
  • Recurring per-person block schedules
  • Arrived-home / left-home notifications

Your data stays yours

100% self-hosted. No cloud. No accounts.

InSpectre runs as three small containers on hardware you control. Nothing about your network — devices, traffic, vulnerabilities — ever leaves your LAN unless you explicitly enable an outbound integration.

  • Built-in auth with JWT sessions & "remember me"
  • Encrypted (AES-256-GCM) backups & restore
  • Optional Home Assistant MQTT auto-discovery
  • Guided first-run setup wizard — no file editing

Spin it up in under a minute

One command gets the whole stack running. Open your browser, finish the setup wizard, and watch your network appear.

bash
# clone & launch
git clone https://github.com/thefunkygibbon/InSpectre.git
cd InSpectre
./inspectre.sh up
